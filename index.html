Edit Desk Article Bookmarklet

Edit a support article with one click!

Installing

  • Make sure your bookmarks toolbar or sidebar is visible (usually found under your browser's View menu).
  • Drag the button below onto your bookmarks toolbar or sidebar.

Using

When you are viewing a support article, click on the bookmark you dragged to your toolbar. A new tab will open to edit the current article on the Desk site.
